About

Founded in 2017 by two Greek immigrants who were world-reknowned chefs in their previous lives, the Towne House captures the quintessential flavors of the Old World with a modern twist.

With a wide variety of specialty souvlakis, wraps, gyros, soups, and salads, you're certain to enjoy the best of what the Mediterranean has to offer.
